Academics

Our teaching methods include: Interactive Smart Classes, Practical & Experiential Learning, and Regular Assessments & Parent-Teacher Meetings.

Curriculum Framework at APS School

At APS School, our curriculum is thoughtfully designed to meet the developmental needs of students at every stage. We combine modern educational tools with age-appropriate learning methods to build a strong academic and emotional foundation for every child.

Nursery

Montessori Play Learning • Sensory Development • Language Exposure

We focus on a joyful, nurturing, and Montessori-based environment to develop motor skills, sensory awareness, and early language through play and exploration.

Primary

STEM Kits • Phonics Program • Project-Based Assessments

We use hands-on STEM kits, a structured phonics program, and project-based assessments to build strong foundations in science, technology, reading, and critical thinking.

Middle

Robotics Lab • Math Olympiad Prep • Career Aptitude Testing (Class 8)

Advanced tools like a robotics lab and Math Olympiad prep challenge analytical thinking. Class 8 includes career aptitude testing for future pathway identification.
